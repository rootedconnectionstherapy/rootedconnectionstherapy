Family Support & Consultation

Children heal best within a supported system. Parent/Carer sessions help you understand your child's emotional world and develop ways of responding that build connection, safety, and co-regulation.

Parent/Carer support can include:

  • reflective parenting conversations
  • co-regulation and connection strategies
  • supporting what's emerging in your child's therapy
  • understanding trauma, attachment and behaviour
  • support for adoptive, foster, and kinship families
  • understanding individual neurodiversity
  • guidance on schools, assessments, referrals, and multi-agency processes

These sessions are collaborative and tailored to your family's unique experience. Together, we develop practical, compassionate approaches tailored to your family's needs.
