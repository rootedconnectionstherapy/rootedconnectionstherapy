Adolescent Therapy (Ages 12 - 18)

I offer a grounded, non-judgemental space where teenagers can talk openly, express creatively, or explore what feels difficult.

Psychotherapy may help with:

  • anxiety, low mood, stress or overwhelm
  • identity, self-esteem and self-understanding
  • friendship or relationship difficulties
  • trauma, loss, or transitions
  • emotional regulation and sensory overwhelm
  • school pressure or academic stress
  • family conflict

My approach balances warmth, steadiness, and gentle challenge. The aim is to help young people feel more connected to themselves and their own internal resources.
